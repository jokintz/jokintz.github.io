Predicting the Academic Performance of a Student from Participation in Activities and Other Factors

CSCI E-107 Final Project

Our task:

Our goal was to explore the relationship between a student's GPA and the activities in which the student takes part.

Our tools:

We used Linear regression to try to predict a student's GPA. We also used Logit functions to try to predict the best students, and several machine learning techniques to attempt to classify students as "at-risk."

Our data:

We used data collected from a student survey given to students at a suburban Indiana High School. Students were asked to rate themselves on many different factors. Some of these included study time, participation in sports/clubs/music, working hours, and reading/gaming hours.
